About Me

From Charlotte, North Carolina originally, I am an aspiring bilingual sports journalist with significant reporting experience, interviewing anyone from high-school athletes to collegiate athletes to Olympians. Though I have some experience with video production, I best thrive in sports writing on print, digital, and online platforms with many bylines for The Daily Orange, The Princeton Review's 2020 top college newspaper. As a dual-major and double minor student at Syracuse University, I’m developing a vast skillset not just in journalism but also in information technology, sport management, and Spanish literature and culture.

Besides my passion for sports and journalism, I love to travel, hoping to have that aspect in my future career as a beat writer for a sports team. I’ve traveled to Peru, Saint Lucia and many other cool places within the United States. In summer 2021, I spent my time studying abroad at Syracuse University at Madrid, Spain, enriching my knowledge of the language while immersing myself in a new culture.
